## What I work on

- **Italian fintech & financial tooling** — legal-interest and monetary-revaluation engines, inflation indices (ISTAT FOI), tax and consumer-finance calculators serving millions of users at tuttosemplice.com
- **Cryptocurrency mining economics** — profitability modelling, difficulty cycles, hardware ROI, halving analysis
- **Blockchain infrastructure** — PoW consensus, mining algorithms (SHA-256, Scrypt, RandomX, kHeavyHash, Equihash), network dynamics
- **AI systems & data tools** — automated data pipelines, LLM-assisted editorial workflows, financial information tools
- **Digital publishing at scale** — building and running editorial operations for millions of readers

My engineering background in control theory and signal modelling gives me a quantitative lens
on crypto networks: difficulty adjustments, hashrate feedback loops, and block reward emission
schedules are, at their core, systems theory problems.
